Can you play Padel in Clapham?

Yes, there are multiple Padel Tennis courts near Clapham and in the surrounding areas. Wandsworth, Battersea, Barnes, and Kingston all have Padel facilities.

Where can you play Padel near Clapham?

The nearest Padel courts to Clapham are:

  • Battersea Sports Centre
  • The Padel Yard Wandsworth
  • Playtime Padel Club Barnes

Battersea Sports Centre

  • Padel Court: Available year-round at Battersea Sports Centre, with one outdoor court.
  • Opening Times:
    • Monday to Friday: 7 AM – 10 PM
    • Saturday to Sunday: 8 AM – 7 PM
    • Bank Holidays: 8 AM – 8 PM
  • Pricing:
    • Peak times (Mon-Fri: 5 PM – 10 PM, Sat-Sun: 8 AM – 7 PM): £50 per hour.
    • Off-peak: £32 per hour.
  • Booking: Available through Enable Leisure.
  • Location: Hope Street, SW11 2DA.

The Padel Yard Wandsworth

  • Location: SW18 1SH
  • Opening Hours: Available throughout the week (exact hours depend on bookings).
  • Courts: 3 indoor and 3 outdoor padel courts.
  • Pricing:
    • £12.50 per hour for non-members
    • £10.50 per hour for Game4Padel members
    • £8.50 per hour for Premium members
  • Facilities: On-site parking, equipment shop, licensed bar, changing facilities.

Playtime Padel Club Barnes

  • Location: Barn Elms Sports Centre, Queen Elizabeth Walk, London SW13 0DG.
  • Facilities: One outdoor doubles court. Equipment is included in the court hire fee, and access to changing facilities and a large free car park is available.
  • Opening Times: Open 7 days a week, during daylight hours only.
  • Pricing: You can book the court via the Padel Mates app. Equipment is included in the price.

Will there be Padel Courts in Clapham in the future?

Yes, Wandsworth Council has given planning permission for courts to be built on the top of the Southside Shopping Centre, not far from Clapham. These should be open by the end of 2024.

What other sport can you play in Clapham?

Clapham has badminton, football, and table tennis facilities, as well as a number of gyms, meaning there are lots of other sports that you can play in Clapham.
